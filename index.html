💧 AI for Water Body Detection

Welcome to this AI mini-course focused on water body identification from remote sensing imagery. The goal is to learn practical machine learning through hands-on lessons.

📘 Lesson 1: Understand Your Data

Before any model training, it's essential to know your data well. Visualization and inspection help uncover structure and potential issues early on.

In this lesson, we explore high-resolution land cover imagery, and create a binary mask: 1 = water, 0 = non-water.
