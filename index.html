üåø Lesson 2: Water Detection with NDWI

In this lesson, we apply the Normalized Difference Water Index (NDWI) technique to identify potential water pixels from imagery.

NDWI enhances water features using green and near-infrared bands:

NDWI = (Green - NIR) / (Green + NIR)

We threshold the NDWI values to create a preliminary water mask.
